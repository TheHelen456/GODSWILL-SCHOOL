ABOUT US

Established in September 2021, God's Will School focuses on academic excellence and also offer comprehensive curriculum that includes ranges of subjects and extracurricular activities. The school's faculty and staff are dedicated to creating a supportive and inclusive environment that fosters growth, creativity and critical thinking. By emphasizing on the importance of values, character and community service, God's Will School seeks to equip its students with the skills and wisdom needed to make a positive impact in the world.

OUR PROGRAMS

  • DAY CARE: Providing a safe, nuturing and engaging environment for children to learn, grow and develop while their parents are at work or otherwise occupied.
  • NURSERY EDUCATION: Fostering a foundation for young children's social, emotional, physical and cognitive development, preparing then for future academic success and lifelong learning.
  • PRIMARY EDUCATION: Building foundational skills in literacy and numeracy and social skills.
  • SECONDARY EDUCATION: Enhancing the foundation laid in primary education and equiping students with advanced knowledge, skills and values necessary for post-secondary education, career pursuits and responsible citizenship.
  • SPECIALIZED TRAINING: Equiping students with specific skills and expertise in particular areas or fields such as Musical Instruments, Handcrafts, Carpentry, etc.
